# 1.8.9 Minecraft Client | Greyss | Made in Under 1 Hour

Drop the `.jar` in your `mods` folder and launch Minecraft 1.8.9.  

⚠️ **Note:** KillAura and Scaffold are detectable on most servers. Use responsibly.  

**Keybinds:**  
- **X** – Toggle KillAura  
- **V** – Hold Scaffold  
- **K** – Toggle ESP  
- **'** – Open GUI
